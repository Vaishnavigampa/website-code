    The menu at Chillis has a wide variety of chicken, meat, seafood, vegetarian and sweet dishes in the styles of Northern India, including Punjab, Rajasthan and Goa cooked to perfection in the Tandoor (Clay Oven). Our breads, rices, appetizers, yogurts and chutneys round out your experience with sweet, savory, tart and spicy flavors.

    Chillis Indian cuisine offers a large selection of lovingly prepared vegetarian dishes that are just as rich and satisfying as our meat dishes. Crispy Bhindi (okra), tender, sweet Saag (spinach), rich Chana (chick peas), buttery Baingen (eggplant) and melt-in-your-mouth Koftas all satisfy your hunger! Our Paneer (cheese) is made fresh daily from pure milk (no soy!) and is the hearty center piece of our Paneer dishes.
